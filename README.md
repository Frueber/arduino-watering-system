# Arduino Watering System  



---

## Introduction  

Trigger relays to power water pumps when soil moisture is low.  

---

## Hardware  

### Microprocessor  

- 1 Arduino Nano.  

### Components  

- 1 (* x) Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor(s) v1.2.  
- 1 Breadboard Power Supply Module 3.3V/5V.  
- 1 4-Channel DC 5V Relay Module.  
- 1 5V DC Water Pump.  

---
